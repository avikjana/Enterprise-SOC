# 📊 SOC Metrics

## 📌 Objective

Measure SOC performance

---

## 📊 Metrics

### 1. MTTD (Mean Time to Detect)

Time taken to detect attack

---

### 2. MTTR (Mean Time to Respond)

Time taken to respond

---

### 3. Alert Volume

Number of alerts generated

---

### 4. Detection Coverage

Mapped to MITRE ATT&CK

---

## 🧠 Why Important?

* Shows SOC efficiency
* used in real companies

---

## ✅ Example

| Metric | Value  |
| ------ | ------ |
| MTTD   | 2 mins |
| MTTR   | 5 mins |
| Alerts | 25     |
